Talks

  • H. Chen, Integrable open spin chain in ABJM theory from giant graviton

    In this talk I will show how the determinant like operators in ABJM theory give rise to an integrable open spin chain. A two-loop level proof of integrability is given by an explicit construction of infinite number of conserved charges. I also conjectured the all loop Bethe ansatz equations which have been checked in the weak coupling limit by comparing with the two-loop scalar sector Bethe equations.

  • Z. Tsuboi, Reflection equation algebras and Baxter Q-operators for open spin chains

    The reflection equation is a fundamental equation in quantum integrable systems with open boundary conditions. I will discuss underlying symmetries of the reflection equation (q-Onsager type algebras) and its solutions (K-matrices). Then I explain how to construct Baxter Q-operators for open spin chains associated with the quantum affine algebra \(U_{q}(\widehat{sl}_2) \).
