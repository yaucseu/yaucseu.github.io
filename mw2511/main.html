International Workshop on QFT and Beyond
量子场论及其拓展国际研讨会

Date: 11/27 - 29 (Thu - Sat), 2025

Location: Shing-Tung Yau Center of Southeast University, Sipailou campus, Nanjing, Jiangsu (江苏省南京市东南大学四牌楼校区） [Google Map]

Introduction

     The aim of this workshop is to bring together experts working on key aspects of quantum field theories, including supersymmetry, integrability, and dualities. We seek to foster lively discussions on new techniques and future directions among all speakers and participants. We also hope everyone will enjoy both the academic atmosphere of our center and the beauty of autumn in Nanjing.
