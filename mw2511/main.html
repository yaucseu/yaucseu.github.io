International Workshop on QFT and Beyond
量子场论及其拓展国际研讨会

Date: 11/27 - 29 (Thu - Sat), 2025

Location: Shing-Tung Yau Center of Southeast University, Sipailou campus, Nanjing, Jiangsu (江苏省南京市东南大学四牌楼校区） [Google Map]

Introduction

     The aim of this workshop, held at Shing-Tung Yau Center of Southeast University is to invite experts working on several aspects of quantum field theories, such as supersymmetry, integrability and dualities and to discuss new powerful techniques and future directions lively interactions among speakers and participants. We also hope that everyone will enjoy both the academic atmosphere of our center and the beauty of autumn in Nanjing.
