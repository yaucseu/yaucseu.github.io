A guide for non-professionals

Shing-Tung Yau wrote a couple of excellent popular science books.

  1. Shing-Tung Yau and Steve Nadis, The Shape of a Life: One Mathematician's Search for the Universe's Hidden Geometry, Yale University Press (2019). Available from Amazon Kindle.
  2. Shing-Tung Yau and Steve Nadis, The Shape of Inner Space: String Theory and the Geometry of the Universe's Hidden Dimensions, Basic Books (2010). Available from Amazon Kindle and Douban

Here are other well-known books on mathematics and physics including string theory.

  1. Graham Farmelo, The Universe Speaks in Numbers: How Modern Maths Reveals Nature's Deepest Secrets, Faber & Faber (2019). Available from Amazon Kindle.
  2. Hirosi Ooguri and Ning Yi (translator), 超弦理论: 探究时间、空间及宇宙的本原, Posts & Telecom Press (2014). Available from Douban.
