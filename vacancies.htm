Faculty Positions

We have received around 100 applications for the year 2020. Thank you very much for your interests.

Shing-Tung Yau Center of Southeast University invites application to faculty positions starting in 2021, in all research areas of theoretical and mathematical physics.

Our center is a new research institute working on mathematics and physics in Nanjing, China. Shing-Tung Yau is the director of our center. The current group members of the physics group are Ryo Suzuki, Wen Qiang and Xu Jianfei. Three more members, Gu Jie, Yunfeng Jiang and Marcus Sperling are expected to join. Researchers in the complementary areas, such as quantum information or statistical physics, are also encouraged to apply. More information can be found on the official page.

This vacancy is an open-rank full-time faculty position. Successful candidates will be appointed as a full professor, associate or assistant professor. We provide a competitive salary, start-up research grant from the university or our center. Proficiency in Mandarin is not required for non-Chinese applicants.

The applicants must submit a cover letter, curriculum vitae, list of publications, research statement, and arrange reference letters through the Academic Jobs Online website. There is no application deadline and open until filled.

Any questions about this job posting should be sent to Ms. Lu Yue <yauc@pub.seu.edu.cn>, Ryo Suzuki <ryosuzuki@seu.edu.cn> or Wen Qiang <wenqiang@seu.edu.cn>.
