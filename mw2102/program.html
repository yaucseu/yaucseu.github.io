Keita Nii (Yukawa), Generalized Giveon-Kutasov duality

In this talk, I will introduce a generalized version of the conventional Giveon-Kutasov duality. This generalization considers adding a fully-generalized Chern-Simons interaction for the U(N) gauge symmetry. The proposed duality unifies the SU(N) and U(N) Chern-Simons matter dualities into a single description. As an application of this duality, I will explain the duality enhancement for the abelian case.
