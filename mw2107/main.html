Southeast Summer Seminar Series

Date: Jul 9, Jul 16, Jul 30, Aug 6 in 2021

Venue: Online by Zoom

Introduction

The main topic of the summer seminar series in 2021 is the mathematical methods for supersymmetric gauge theory and string theory. In order to raise awareness on the rapidly developping theoretical concepts as well as their physical application, we organize a series of seminars by inviting experts of each field and stimulate interactions.

    All seminars are given online. Participants are not needed to register in advance. The talks are by invitation only. The information about the past seminar series can be found here.
